Get to Know Us

Ndege Insurance Brokers (NIB) Limited
Ndege Insurance was honored to be the first Tanzanian agent to qualify and registered as Insurance broker in Tanzania. For over 50 Years of experience, we have gathered a vast network of Insurance and reinsurance partners all around the world, with Capabilities to arrange placement of complex risks locally and across the global market. We are authorized representatives for BMS GLOBAL REINSURANCE BROKER, CHARTERD BROKERS INSURANCE and First Reinsurance Brokers Ltd
