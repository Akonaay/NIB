GET TO KNOW US

Ndege Insurance Brokers (NIB) Limited

Ndege Insurance Brokers was honored to be the first Tanzanian agent to qualify and registered as Insurance broker in Tanzania. For over 50 Years of experience, we have gathered a vast network of Insurance and reinsurance partners all around the world, with Capabilities to arrange placement of complex risks locally and across the global market.
Ndege Insurance Brokers has grown into a well respected insurance brokarage services provider in Tanzania. The Company has outstanding experience in providing quality packages in Health care and General Insurances and has been providing services for the past 50 years. Above all, the company is proud to be 100% locally owned and managed, among the few large insurance brokers firms in Tanzania.
With our network branches in Dar es salaam ( Morani House ) Mwanza and Musoma. Ndege Insurance has managed to offer Insurance products to wide client bases. Our company currently serves the insurance need of over 2,500 individuals as well as over 45 different business sectors/companies. More important, the company has been improving its market share in its core insurance business while successfully branching out into different Tanzanian Markets.
